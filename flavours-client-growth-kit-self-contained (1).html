Product concept

The product is a Flavours Client Growth Kit: a full implementation pack that gives the business a workable retention and referral structure without requiring a complex CRM rollout on day one.

Core outcome

Standardise what currently happens informally

Move from ad hoc thank-yous and organic word-of-mouth to a managed system with timing, segmentation, templates, tracking, and measurement.

Operating workflow

Stage 1

After every event

  • Mark client as corporate or private.
  • Log event type, date, value, and contact owner.
  • Record any dietary or service notes worth reusing.
Stage 2

Within 24–72 hours

  • Send thank-you message.
  • Request quick feedback.
  • If satisfaction is positive, open the referral prompt.
Stage 3

Next-booking activation

  • Corporate: apply spend milestone or account benefit.
  • Private: send event reminder or seasonal inspiration.
  • Set a next-action date for follow-up.

Suggested programme rules

Corporate model

Simple partnership programme

  • After defined annual spend, unlock better pricing or service perks.
  • Referral reward = credit on a future order after referred booking is completed.
  • Optional tier labels for internal use: Core, Preferred, Premium.
Private model

Light-touch celebration programme

  • Returning client receives a small next-booking discount or a menu extra.
  • Referral thank-you stays modest and optional, such as a warm acknowledgement or small perk.
  • Use calendar-style reminders around birthdays, anniversaries, and holiday periods.
